Test drive

The main aim of the course is to develop language skills in English while at the same time studying philosophical problems around film.

Your philosophical study will be guided by two broad kinds of problems: philosophical problems in films or motivated by them and philosophical problems about film as a way to philosophize.

Your language skills in English will be developed by the active use of the language in written and oral discussion, writing of short texts and understanding lectures, films and texts in English.
